Pool heating

Keep 17.8 kL at 20°C

The pool is 2.7 m x 4.7 m x 1.4 m, about 17,766 litres. Heating that water by 1°C needs 20.7 kWh of heat. With a pool heat pump, that is roughly 3.4-5.2 kWh of electricity per 1°C depending on real COP.
